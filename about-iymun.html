About IYMUN

Introduction

The Incheon Youth Model United Nations (IYMUN) is a Model United Nations conference designed specifically for begineer delegates. Hosted by KYLO, our mission is to provide an accessible, beginner-friendly, and meaningful MUN experience that empowers students to develop leadership skills.

Furthermore, all profits will be donated to a medical initiative. Through participation in IYMUN, delegates not only engage in debate and diplomacy, but also contribute to a cause that extends beyond the conference itself.

Why IYMUN?

  • High-quality conference organized by award-winning delegates and experienced chairs
  • Welcoming environment where both beginner and experienced delegates can thrive
  • Profits dedicated to donating medical supplies to a medical facility
  • Affordable participation with low financial barrier

Registration Eligibility

  • Middle/High school students
  • Must be proficient in English
  • No prior experience in MUN required
