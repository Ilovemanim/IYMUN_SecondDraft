About IYMUN

What is IYMUN?

IYMUN (International Youth Model United Nations) is an annual Model United Nations conference hosted for middle and high school students. Our mission is to provide an authentic diplomatic simulation where young people can develop critical thinking, public speaking, and negotiation skills while engaging with pressing global issues.

Our Conference

IYMUN XIV represents the fourteenth iteration of our prestigious conference. Each year, we attract hundreds of delegates from schools across the region and internationally. Our conference follows authentic UN procedures and protocols, providing delegates with a genuine diplomatic experience.

Educational Objectives

Diplomatic Skills

Learn negotiation, consensus-building, and diplomatic communication in a realistic setting.

Global Awareness

Deepen understanding of international relations, global challenges, and UN operations.

Public Speaking

Develop confidence and eloquence in expressing ideas to diverse audiences.

Critical Thinking

Analyze complex global issues from multiple perspectives and formulate evidence-based positions.

Delegate Experience

As a delegate at IYMUN, you will:

  • Represent an assigned country in one of our specialized committees
  • Research pressing international issues relevant to your committee's agenda
  • Engage in constructive debate with fellow delegates
  • Draft, negotiate, and vote on resolutions
  • Network with young leaders from diverse backgrounds
  • Experience the thrill of international diplomacy

Why Choose IYMUN?

  • Authentic Experience: Our conference strictly adheres to UN rules of procedure
  • Expert Facilitation: Experienced chairs and moderators ensure productive debates
  • Diverse Committees: Multiple committee options catering to different interests
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with driven students from around the world
  • Personal Growth: Develop skills that will benefit you throughout your life
  • Memorable Experience: Join thousands of alumni who treasure their IYMUN memories
