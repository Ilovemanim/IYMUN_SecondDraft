WHO

WHO Introduction

The World Health Organization, established in 1948, is dedicated to directing and coordinating international efforts to achieve the highest possible level of health for all people. WHO works with governments and partners to address health emergencies, improve maternal and child health, combat infectious diseases, and promote healthy lifestyles.

WHO Agenda

Agenda A

Balancing Antimicrobial Resistance Control Measures with Equitable Access to Essential Medicines

Agenda B

Ensuring Equitable Access to Prevention and Treatment for Climate-Sensitive Infectious Diseases in Vulnerable Regions
