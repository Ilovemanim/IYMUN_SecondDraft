Position Paper

A position paper is MANDATORY for award consideration in this MUN. Delegates must submit a position paper by August 7th. The position paper is a 1-2 page report that delegates must write regarding their country stances on the agenda. Position papers must be submitted as a PDF file through gmail: koreanyouth@gmail.com with the heading "[Committee_Name]_[Full Name]_Position Paper" ex: WHO_Jihoon Choi_Position Paper
