Position Paper

To maintain a high-quality conference, a position paper is mandatory for award consideration in IYMUN. Delegates must submit a position paper by August 7th after their country allocation in July 31st. The position paper is a 1-2 page report that delegates must write regarding their country stances on the agenda. The position paper guide can be accessed through the link below. AI-written position papers are banned and if found, will substantially affect the delegate's ability to earn awards.
