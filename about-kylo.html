About KYLO

Learn about the organization behind IYMUN

KYLO Organization

KYLO (Korean Youth Leaders Organization) is a prestigious organization dedicated to nurturing the next generation of global leaders through educational initiatives and cultural exchange programs.

Our History

Founded in 2010, KYLO has been instrumental in developing youth leadership programs across Asia. With over a decade of experience, we have successfully organized numerous international conferences, workshops, and seminars that have impacted thousands of students.

Our Vision

We envision a world where young leaders from diverse backgrounds collaborate to solve global challenges through dialogue, understanding, and mutual respect. KYLO is committed to fostering international cooperation and cultural appreciation among youth.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: Commitment to delivering high-quality educational experiences
  • Inclusivity: Welcoming youth from all backgrounds and experiences
  • Integrity: Upholding ethical standards in all our programs
  • Innovation: Continuously improving our programs and methodologies
  • Leadership: Empowering young people to lead with purpose

Key Initiatives

Beyond IYMUN, KYLO operates several flagship programs including international youth exchanges, leadership seminars, and community service projects. Each initiative is designed to build capacity and foster global citizenship among participants.

Global Impact

Through our programs, KYLO has connected over 10,000 young leaders across 50+ countries. Our alumni have gone on to pursue careers in international relations, diplomacy, humanitarian work, and public service.
