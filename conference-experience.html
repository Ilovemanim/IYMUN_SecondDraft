Conference Experience

What Awaits You at IYMUN XIV

An Unforgettable Experience

IYMUN XIV is designed to provide delegates with a comprehensive and immersive diplomatic experience. From the moment you register to the closing ceremony, every element of the conference is carefully crafted to maximize your learning and enjoyment.

What to Expect

Opening Ceremony

The conference begins with an inspiring opening ceremony featuring distinguished speakers, including diplomats and international relations experts. This sets the tone for an intellectually stimulating conference and welcomes delegates from around the world.

Committee Sessions

Delegates participate in multiple committee sessions throughout the conference, engaging in formal debates, moderated caucuses, and unmoderated caucuses. Each session builds toward the resolution voting, where delegates must persuade colleagues to support their positions.

Networking Opportunities

Beyond formal sessions, IYMUN provides numerous opportunities to connect with fellow delegates from different countries and backgrounds. Breaks, informal caucuses, and social events facilitate meaningful conversations and friendships.

Special Programming

Delegate Workshops

Skill-building sessions on topics like diplomacy, public speaking, research methodology, and conference navigation

Expert Panels

Interactive discussions with international relations professionals and diplomats from various sectors

Mentor Programs

First-time delegates are paired with experienced MUN veterans to provide guidance and support

Awards Ceremony

Recognition of outstanding delegates, awards for best resolutions, and special commendations

Social Events

IYMUN XIV features a gala dinner where delegates can celebrate their hard work and build connections in a relaxed setting. This is an opportunity to make memories with new friends from around the world and reflect on the conference.

Accommodation & Support

We've partnered with nearby hotels to provide comfortable accommodation for out-of-town delegates. Support staff is available throughout the conference to assist with any questions or concerns. Detailed information about accommodation will be provided upon registration.

Alumni Success

IYMUN alumni have gone on to pursue careers in diplomacy, international relations, law, policy, business, and many other fields. The skills and connections developed at IYMUN have proven invaluable to our participants' personal and professional development.

Delegate Testimonials
