About Model United Nations

What is MUN?

Model United Nations is an educational simulation where students act as delegates representing countries in the United Nations. Participants work together to solve global problems through diplomatic negotiation, resolution drafting, and formal debate.

Why MUN?

MUN helps students build public speaking, research, critical thinking, and leadership skills. It also deepens understanding of international affairs, the United Nations, and how global cooperation can shape a better future.

Process of MUN

  1. Delegates are assigned a country and committee.
  2. They prepare position papers on the committee agenda.
  3. Debate begins with opening speeches and moderated caucuses.
  4. Delegates negotiate, draft working papers, and shape resolutions.
  5. The committee votes on final resolutions at the end.
