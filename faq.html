Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about IYMUN XIV

General Questions

What is Model United Nations?

Model United Nations is an educational simulation in which students learn about diplomacy, international relations, and the United Nations. Participants take on roles as delegates of countries and engage in realistic UN procedures to address global issues.

Do I need prior MUN experience to participate?

No! IYMUN welcomes both first-time participants and experienced delegates. We provide beginner-friendly resources and mentor programs to help newcomers succeed.

What age groups can participate?

IYMUN XIV is open to middle school students (grades 6-9) and high school students (grades 9-12). School delegations and individual delegates are both welcome.

Registration & Fees

When does registration close?

Regular registration closes on November 15, 2026. Late registration is available until November 22 with an additional fee. We encourage early registration to secure your preferred committee.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, bank transfers, and other online payment methods through our secure registration portal. Contact us for alternative payment arrangements if needed.

Is there a group discount?

Yes! School delegations of 5 or more delegates receive a 10% discount on registration fees. Contact our registration team for details.

What if I need to cancel?

Refunds are available according to our refund policy: 50% refund before November 1, 25% refund until November 15, and no refund after November 15.

Conference Details

Where is IYMUN XIV being held?

The conference will be held at KAIST International Hall in Daejeon, South Korea. More details are available on the Venue page.

What should I bring to the conference?

Bring formal attire for the gala dinner, notebook and pen for committee sessions, your country briefing materials, and any other research you want to reference. We'll provide delegate packets with additional materials.

Are meals provided?

Yes, all meals and snacks throughout the conference are included in your registration fee. We accommodate dietary restrictions; please indicate these during registration.

How many committees will there be?

IYMUN XIV will feature two committees: WHO (World Health Organization) and UNCSTD (UN Commission on Science and Technology for Development).

Preparation & Committees

How should I prepare for the conference?

Research your assigned country's policies and positions on the committee agenda items. Review the committee topic guides on our website, and familiarize yourself with UN procedures.

Can I choose which country I represent?

During registration, you can indicate your country preference. Countries are assigned based on preferences and balance across the committee, though preference cannot be guaranteed.

Can I choose my committee?

Yes! You can select your preferred committee during registration. If capacity is limited, committee placement may be subject to availability.

What is a position paper?

A position paper outlines your assigned country's stance on the committee agenda items. Guidelines and templates are available on our website. Position papers are not mandatory but highly recommended.

Logistics & Accommodations

Is accommodation provided?

Accommodation is not included in the registration fee, but we have partnerships with nearby hotels offering discounted rates for IYMUN delegates. Details will be sent to registered participants.

How do I get to the venue?

The venue is easily accessible by train and car. Detailed directions and transportation information will be provided to all registered delegates. Airport shuttle services may also be available.

Will there be parking available?

Yes, on-site parking is available for school delegations arriving by car. Please indicate if you need parking during registration.
