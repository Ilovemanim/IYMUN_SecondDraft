Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: Can delegates choose their own country?

No. Although participants can choose their committee, country allocation will be random. Note that an email regarding country allocation and other preperation material will be sent by 7/31.

Q: Is the orientation session mandatory to join?

Yes. The orientation session on 8/1 covers basic rules of procedures, introduces committee, and allows delegates to form networks. After the orientation, an non-mandatory education session will be held, where chairs will be giving tips for new delegates.

Q: Where will the registration fee be used for?

The delegation fee is used to purchase conference materials. Furthermore, all profit will be donated to the KYMI project. A public release detailing financial transactions will be released on our social media.

Q: Where can I contact for inqueries?

If your question isn't answered above, please don't hesitate to contact us!
