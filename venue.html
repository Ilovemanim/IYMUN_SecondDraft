Conference Venue

Venue Location

Name: Seo-gu Youth Center

Address: 인천광역시 서구 원창로 92 (원창동)

Why Seo-gu Youth Center?

MUN conferences are often held in prestigious venues such as university campuses and conference centers. While these locations may enhance the conference's reputation, they can also increase the financial burden on participating delegates. In line with IYMUN's mission of making Model United Nations accessible to all students, the conference was held at Seo-gu Youth Center, a public institution that offers a high-quality venue while helping to keep participation costs affordable.

Parking

Seogu-Youth Center offers a wide parking space. However, plese note that the parking space may get cramped. Thus, we recommend that parents drop off their child at the front gate or ride a public transit.
