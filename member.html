Letter from the Secretary-General

Esteemed delegates and guests,

Welcome to the Korean Youth Online Model United Nations 2026! My name is Jihoon Choi, secretary-general of KYOMUN. I am excited to introduce KYOMUN, hosted by KYLO and held virtually from August 10th to August 12th.

The core mission of KYOMUN simple. We aim to provide an accessible, yet high-quality MUN conference. After realizing the challenge beginner delegates face we envisioned a conference where delegates of all levels could thrive. To achieve this mission, we are providing a MUN educational session and individual feedback for delegates in addition to our conference.

We hope to open the doors of MUN to everyone. This committment has become even more valuable with the normalization of AI. MUN has remained as one of the few activities where AI-generated content is ineffective. The human attempts at persuation, moving the masses through public speaking, and creatively thinking about global issues is something AI can not generate.

Our secretariats have worked tirelessly to provide the best MUN experience. If you are someone who feels strongly about global issues, we welcome you to join KYOMUN!

Thank you.

Jihoon Choi, Secretary-General
